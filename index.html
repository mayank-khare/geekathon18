WHAT IS GEEKATHON'18?

A hackathon which believes that programming can solves the problems.

Geek-a-thon is a zonal-level coding competition that is going to be conducted by Programming Club OGI, in Bhopal during Tech Acme 2K18. It is 2 phase competition - a Zonal Round (online) on Skillenza on February 3, and the Final Round (offline) at OIST Bhopal on the 2nd day of Tech Acme 2K18.

What are the Perks?

Community Creation

Meeting new people and engaging actively in this amazing process of innovation combined with competition helps in creating communities. GeekAthon stands on the front-end of it.

Prizes for Winners

The Winners of the Final Round will be awarded by our Partners of the event. Prizes worth Lakhs of rupees, and consequential internship opportunities in notable startups and companies.

Innovation

GeekAthon will acts as a hub of innovation for the participants intrigued by the problems of diurnal life, and know that programming can provide solutions.

FAQs


What is a hackathon?
A hackathon is a marathon software and hardware product creation event in which you have to develop Applications or website on a given particular theme in a span of 24 Hours.
I’ve never been to a hackathon and can I take part?
Have no fear it will be a fun learning experience! We will have beginner workshops held by Extreme Club on web design, mobile applications, backends, cloud content management, and more! Our goal is everyone should know how to present their ideas using coding skills.

Who can attend?
Any undergraduate or graduate student at any university in the world!
What should I bring?
Bring your own College IDs, Laptops, chargers, wires, cameras, hardware or IoT devices along with your passion, ideas, skills and creativity.

Will this cost me money?
Yes! A minimal cost of Rs. 50. But, We’ll provide all swag, and fun times you need to have an amazing weekend.
